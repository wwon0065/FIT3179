The World Happiness Report is a publication of the United Nations Sustainable Development Solutions Network that reports how people evaluate their happiness level in more than 150 countries worldwide. It has measured the happiness score based on several attributes such as dystopia, GDP per capita, healthy life expectancy, social support, freedom to make life choices, generosity, and perceptions of business corruption. This report has gain global recognition as governments, organizations and civil society increasingly make use of these reports to inform their policy-making decisions. It has reminded the world to pursue for happiness despite the pandemic and ills of disease and war we must tackle with.

Source of Dataset: World Happiness Report 2015-2022 and World Happiness Report 2022
